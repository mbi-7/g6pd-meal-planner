🛒 Grocery Checklist

Proteins
  • Chicken breast: 1.2 kg
  • Salmon: 500 g
  • Cod: 400 g
  • Tuna (canned in water): 3 cans
  • Turkey breast: 400 g
Vegetables
  • Broccoli: 2 heads
  • Sweet potatoes: 4 medium
  • Zucchini: 3
  • Tomatoes: 5

📚 Recipe Collection

🧂 Ingredients:
  • 400g cod fillet
  • 2 sweet potatoes
  • Olive oil, herbs
📋 Instructions:
  1. Bake cod at 200°C for 15–20 minutes.
  2. Boil and mash sweet potatoes with olive oil and herbs.

🧑‍🍳 Meal Prep Tips

  • Prep grains like quinoa and rice in batches and refrigerate.
  • Boil eggs in advance for quick protein.
  • Chop veggies ahead and store in containers.

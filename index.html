📚 Recipe Collection

🧂 Ingredients:
  • 150g salmon fillet
  • 1 medium sweet potato
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper
  • Lemon slices (optional)
📋 Instructions:
  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Peel and dice sweet potato, toss with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place sweet potatoes on baking tray and bake for 10 minutes.
  4. Add salmon (seasoned) and broccoli to the tray. Drizzle with oil.
  5. Bake another 12–15 minutes until salmon is cooked through.
  6. Serve with lemon slices.
🧂 Ingredients:
  • 150g chicken breast
  • 1/2 cup quinoa
  • 1 cup mixed greens
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt & pepper
  • Lemon juice
📋 Instructions:
  1. Cook quinoa according to package instructions.
  2. Season chicken with salt, pepper, and cumin. Grill until cooked through.
  3. Toss greens with olive oil and lemon juice.
  4. Serve chicken over quinoa and greens.
🧂 Ingredients:
  • 1 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup mixed berries
  • 1/4 cup oats
  • 1 tsp honey (optional)
📋 Instructions:
  1. Layer Greek yogurt, oats, and berries in a bowl.
  2. Top with a drizzle of honey if desired.
🧂 Ingredients:
  • 1 cup cooked lentils
  • 1/2 onion (chopped)
  • 1 garlic clove (minced)
  • 1 carrot (diced)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • Salt, pepper, cumin
  • 1 whole wheat pita
📋 Instructions:
  1. Sauté onion, garlic, and carrot in olive oil until soft.
  2. Add lentils, broth, and spices. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  3. Blend if desired for a creamy texture.
  4. Serve with warm whole wheat pita.
🧂 Ingredients:
  • 150g shrimp (peeled)
  • 1 zucchini
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, turmeric
📋 Instructions:
  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Chop vegetables and toss with olive oil and spices.
  3. Spread on a baking tray and roast 15 minutes.
  4. Add shrimp on top and roast another 8–10 minutes until cooked.
  5. Serve hot.
🧂 Ingredients:
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped spinach
  • 1 slice whole grain toast
  • Salt, pepper
  • 1 tsp olive oil
📋 Instructions:
  1. Whisk eggs with salt and pepper.
  2. Sauté vegetables in olive oil, then pour eggs over.
  3. Cook until set, flipping once.
  4. Serve with toast.
🧂 Ingredients:
  • 150g lamb (lean cut)
  • 1 medium sweet potato
  • 1 zucchini
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, rosemary
📋 Instructions:
  1. Peel, chop, and boil sweet potato until soft. Mash with olive oil and salt.
  2. Sear lamb in a pan with salt, pepper, and rosemary until desired doneness.
  3. Slice and sauté zucchini until soft.
  4. Serve together.
🧂 Ingredients:
  • 300g ground turkey
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup oats
  • 1/2 onion (grated)
  • 1 garlic clove (minced)
  • Salt & pepper
  • 1 tsp cumin
📋 Instructions:
  1. Preheat oven to 190°C (375°F).
  2. Mix all ingredients in a bowl and form into small balls.
  3. Place on a baking tray lined with parchment paper.
  4. Bake for 20–25 minutes until golden and cooked through.
  5. Serve with brown rice or salad.
🧂 Ingredients:
  • 1 slice whole grain bread
  • 1/2 ripe avocado
  • 1 boiled or poached egg
  • Salt, pepper
  • Chili flakes (optional)
📋 Instructions:
  1. Toast the bread.
  2. Mash avocado and spread over toast.
  3. Top with egg, season with salt and pepper.
  4. Sprinkle chili flakes if desired.
🧂 Ingredients:
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup oats
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp olive oil (for cooking)
📋 Instructions:
  1. Blend banana, eggs, oats, and cinnamon until smooth.
  2. Heat olive oil in a non-stick pan.
  3. Pour batter into small circles and cook until bubbles form.
  4. Flip and cook until golden on both sides.
  5. Serve plain or with a few berries.
🧂 Ingredients:
  • 1 can tuna (in water)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Lettuce leaves or whole grain wrap
  • 1 small cucumber (chopped)
  • 1 small tomato (chopped)
  • Salt, pepper
📋 Instructions:
  1. Drain tuna and mix with chopped vegetables and olive oil.
  2. Season to taste.
  3. Wrap in lettuce leaves or a whole grain tortilla.
  4. Serve cold.
🧂 Ingredients:
  • 2 cod fillets (about 400g total)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried thyme or oregano
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 lemon (sliced)
  • 2 medium sweet potatoes
  • 1 tbsp olive oil (for mash)
📋 Instructions:
  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Peel and chop sweet potatoes, then boil in water until soft (about 20 minutes).
  3. Place cod fillets on a baking dish, drizzle with olive oil, season with herbs, salt, pepper, and lemon slices.
  4. Bake cod for 12–15 minutes or until it flakes easily.
  5. Mash the boiled sweet potatoes with olive oil, salt, and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve cod over mashed sweet potato.

🧑‍🍳 Meal Prep Tips

  • Prep grains like quinoa and brown rice in batches and refrigerate.
  • Boil eggs and store them in the fridge for easy breakfasts or snacks.
  • Make a batch of lentil soup or turkey meatballs and freeze portions.
  • Chop vegetables (like broccoli, zucchini, carrots) ahead of time.
  • Use airtight containers and label them with prep dates.
